Kitchen

Sustainable solutions for your kitchen—from reusable paper towels to plastic-free cleaning supplies

Papaya Reusables

Started by husband and wife who noticed after having their first daughter how bulky, expensive, and wasteful paper towels were. Determined to find an alternative which led them to creating Papaya. The reusable towels are made out of 70% cellulose and 30% cotton and are 100% compostable. They are machine washable and dishwasher safe.

Products: Reusable paper towels, reusable bags

Silpat

Silicone baking mats that replace parchment paper. Parchment paper helps savory dishes and sweet treats slide off the pan easily but is single-use and hard to compost or recycle. A silicon baking mat is a great alternative and is easy to clean.
